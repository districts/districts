About District Finder

District Finder is a simple, responsive application and hosted service for locating which district or zone an address falls within. Many municipalities and service providers maintain service districts, zones, or other polygon boundaries, which define their service areas. District Finder aims to provide an easy solution for stakeholders who find themselves asking "Which district am I in?", "Where do I go to vote?", or "What day will my recycling be collected?".

Simply provide your polygon boundary data in geojson, or topojson format, and have a working "finder" application up and running in minutes. Provide your users with the URL link or embed the application on your own website or blog.

How It Works

District Finder is a responsive client-side web application, which can be accessed from any modern web browser, from a desktop computer to a tablet or smartphone. It is built on the Google Maps JavaScript API, the Turf spatial analysis library, and Materialize, a modern responsive front-end framework based on Material Design principles.

  • Depending on your configuration settings, when the user visits your "finder" site, they can be immediately geolocated and a point-in-polygon analysis will determine which district they are currently within, providing them with additional information.
  • Users can search for an address, which will find the corresponding coordinates, locate the address on the map, and determine which district the address falls within.
  • Users can also move the map marker to another location on the map to view the corresponding address and district information for that location as well.
